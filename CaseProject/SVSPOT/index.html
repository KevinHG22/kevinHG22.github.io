Popular Dishes

PASTELITOS

Looking for a delicious and authentic Salvadoran dish? Look no further than pastelitos de carne! These crispy empanadas are filled with a tantalizing mixture of minced meat and vegetables, making them the perfect snack or main course. With their traditional flavor and one-of-a-kind texture, pastelitos de carne are sure to become a new favorite in your household!

PUPUSAS

When it comes to authentic Salvadorian cuisine, pupusas are definitely a must-try! These delicious thick griddle cakes are made with either cornmeal or rice flour, and are stuffed with a variety of fillings like cheese, beans, pork, and even vegetables. Pupusas are then cooked on a comal (a type of griddle) until they're nice and crispy.

EMPANADAS

Looking for an exotic snack that's both sweet and savory? Look no further than empanadas from El Salvador! These delectable little pies are made with plantains, which give them a uniquely sweet flavor. Despite their sweetness, plantains are actually relatively low in sugar, so you can enjoy these empanadas guilt-free. Whether you're snacking on the go or looking for a party appetizer, empanadas from El Salvador are sure to please!

Popular Drinks

ATOL DE ELOTE

Looking for a delicious way to warm up this winter? Atol de elote is the perfect solution! This traditional Salvadoran beverage is made with corn kernels, cinnamon, and milk, making it thick, sweet, and absolutely irresistible. Perfect for breakfast or snacks, Atol de elote is also the perfect complement to tamales. So don't wait any longer, give Atol de elote a try today!

KOLASHANPAN

Kolashanpan is a delicious carbonated beverage from El Salvador that will tantalize your taste buds! It comes in three convenient forms - glass bottle, plastic bottle, or can - so you can enjoy it wherever you are. Whether you're relaxing at home or on the go, Kolashanpan is the perfect drink to quench your thirst. Created by Embotelladora La Cascada, this refreshing beverage is sure to become your new favorite.

HORCHATA

Salvadoran horchata is a delicious, refreshing drink that is perfect for any occasion! Made with morro seeds, squash seeds, white rice, sesame seeds, peanuts, cocoa beans, cinnamon, nutmeg, coriander seeds and allspice, this delightful beverage is sure to please everyone. Sweetened with sugar and vanilla extract, horchata is the perfect drink to cool down with on a hot summer day. Serve it at your next party or gathering, and see how quickly it becomes everyone's favorite!
